Masala Dosa

A crispy, savory crepe from South India.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup Urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp Fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or Ghee for cooking
  • Potato Masala (for filling)

Directions

  1. Soak rice and dal separately for 4-6 hours. Grind to a smooth batter.
  2. Ferment the batter overnight in a warm place. Add salt.
  3. Heat a tawa (griddle). Pour a ladle of batter and spread it thin.
  4. Drizzle oil/ghee around the edges. Cook until golden brown.
  5. Add potato masala in the center and fold the dosa.
  6. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
