Gajar Ka Halwa

A traditional Indian carrot pudding dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram grated carrot
  • 20 grams raisins
  • 2 tablespoon ghee
  • 250 grams condensed milk
  • 25 grams cashews
  • 2 cups milk
  • 5 strands saffron

Directions

  1. Prepare saffron milk: In a small bowl, add a tablespoon of warming milk and Kesar strands and keep it aside.
  2. Boil grated carrot: In a kadhai (pan), mix the milk and carrots together and on a low flame slowly bring them to a boil. Roast nuts separately.
  3. Cook with condensed milk: After a boil, add Kesar flakes. Boil till milk dries up. Add condensed milk and stir till dry.
  4. Final touches: Add ghee and cook for 10 minutes. Garnish with raisins and cashews. Serve hot.
