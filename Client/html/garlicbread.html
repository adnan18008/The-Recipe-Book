Garlic Bread

Crispy, buttery, and full of garlic flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 Baguettes
  • 10 cloves minced garlic
  • 250 gm butter
  • Handful of chopped mint/parsley leaves

Directions

  1. Mix butter, minced garlic, and chopped leaves in a bowl.
  2. Slice the baguettes (not all the way through).
  3. Spread the herb butter generousy on every slice.
  4. Wrap in foil and bake at 200°C for 5-6 minutes.
  5. Remove foil and bake until crisp (5-6 more mins).
  6. Garnish and serve warm.
