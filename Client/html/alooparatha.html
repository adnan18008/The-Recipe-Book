Aloo Paratha

Popular Indian stuffed bread with spiced potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 6 potatoes (boiled)
  • 1 green chilli
  • 10 coriander leaves
  • Red chilli powder to taste
  • 4 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger
  • 1/2 tablespoon carom seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups wheat flour

Directions

  1. Boil potatoes, peel and mash them.
  2. Add chilli powder, salt, green chillies, ginger, coriander, carom seeds to mashed potatoes. Mix well.
  3. Knead wheat flour with water and salt to a soft dough.
  4. Roll a small portion of dough, stuff with potato mixture, seal and roll again.
  5. Cook on a hot tawa with ghee until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve hot with butter and curd.
