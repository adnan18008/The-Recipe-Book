Butter Chicken

Rich and creamy chicken curry.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 1 kg Tomatoes
  • Butter, Cream, Oil
  • Spices: Chilli powder, Tandoori masala, Salt, Sugar
  • Ginger-Garlic paste, Green chillies
  • Dried Fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi)
  • Thick curd for marination

Directions

  1. Marinate chicken with curd, spices, and ginger-garlic paste for 4-5 hours.
  2. Cook marinated chicken in oil until dry/crispy.
  3. Boil tomatoes, blend to puree and strain.
  4. Heat butter, saute ginger-garlic, chillies. Add tomato puree and cook.
  5. Add chicken pieces, salt, sugar. Cover and cook 15 mins.
  6. Finish with fenugreek leaves and fresh cream. Serve hot.
