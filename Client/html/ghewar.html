Rajasthani Ghewar

A honeycomb-shaped sweet delicacy.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup flour
  • 4 tsp ghee
  • 3/4 cup cold milk, 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 tsp Lemon juice
  • Syrup: 1 cup Sugar, 1 cup Water, Cardamom, Saffron
  • Topping: Rabri/Malai, Almonds, Pistachios, Silver leaf

Directions

  1. Make sugar syrup (1 string consistency).
  2. Churn ghee and ice till white. Add flour and milk/water to make a very thin cold batter.
  3. Heat ghee in a deep pan with a ring mold.
  4. Pour batter in thin stream from height into the center. Repeat till honeycomb forms.
  5. Fry till golden. Remove and drain oil.
  6. Dip in sugar syrup. Top with Rabri and nuts.
