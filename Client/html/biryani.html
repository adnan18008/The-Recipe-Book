Chicken Biryani

A flavorful rice dish with chicken, spices, and aromatics.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled basmati rice
  • 600 gm chicken
  • 2 teaspoons virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon ghee
  • Whole spices: Cardamom, Clove, Bay leaf, Cinnamon
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • Ginger-Garlic paste, Green chillies
  • Spices: Turmeric, Garam Masala, Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 cup Hung Curd
  • Saffron, Rose water, Kewra water
  • Fresh Mint and Coriander leaves

Directions

  1. Prepare saffron water and kewra water. Chop onions and herbs.
  2. Heat oil, saute whole spices and onions. Add chicken and powdered spices/curd. Cook for 2-3 mins.
  3. Add garam masala, herbs, and aromatics. Cook chicken till tender.
  4. Layer cooked rice over chicken. Add saffron water and ghee.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat (Dum) for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fried onions and serve hot with raita.
